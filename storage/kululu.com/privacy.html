Privacy Policy

This policy is effective as of May 15th 2023.

Kululu (“we”, “us”, or “our”) is operated by Crunchy Granola LLC. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy (“Policy”) explains how we collect, use, store, and share your data when you use our services via our website.

Please take a moment to read our Policy, as well as our Terms of Service. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to email us at hello@kululu.me. If you do not agree with this Policy do not access or use our Services.

If we make any major changes in this Policy, we will send you an e-mail with the new version, but we insist, come visit us from time to time to keep yourself updated about it.

Key points of this term:

1. Definitions

  • “Services” means our platform features, including wedding event tools such as RSVP websites, speech and vow generators, albums, and planning resources.
  • “Website” refers to https://www.kululu.com.
  • “Personal Data” means data that identifies you as a person (e.g. name, email, uploaded content).
  • “Data” refers to any information collected from users, including usage and device data.
  • “Processing” means any operation performed on personal data (e.g. collecting, storing).
  • “Controller” means Crunchy Granola LLC, which decides how and why your data is processed.
  • “Processor” means a third party that processes data on our behalf.
  • “GDPR” refers to the General Data Protection Regulation (EU).
  • “CCPA” refers to the California Consumer Privacy Act.
  • “Australian Privacy Act” means the Privacy Act 1988 (AU).

2. What Data We Collect

All the information required and processed by us, as the controllers, are adequate, limited and necessary for our Services. We collect Data in different ways and use different technical means. To explain in a more transparent/easy way we divided the aspect of Data in two ways such as appointed on definitions topic above:

Personal Data (provided by you):

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Uploaded content (e.g. images, videos)
  • Other information you choose to provide when using our services, such as messages, preferences, or form inputs, which may be necessary to deliver features, personalize your experience, or provide support.


Device and Usage Data (collected automatically):

We use cookies and similar technologies to enable essential functionality and enhance user experience. You may disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect service performance.

3. Subprocessors We Use
To operate Kululu effectively, we use the following subprocessors who may process some of your data. All third parties are GDPR/CCPA-compliant. We only share your data with these subprocessors for the specific services they provide to us, and under data protection agreements.

  • Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) – Used for backend services including hosting, real-time database, authentication, and storage of  content, e.g., uploaded photos and videos. (US)
  • Simple Analytics – Used to understand anonymous user behavior on our marketing website (EU - NL).
  • Mixpanel – Used for analytics to monitor feature usage,  flows, and to optimize onboarding and engagement (US).
  • Mailgun – Used for sending transactional emails such as welcome emails, account verification, and event updates (US).
  • Intercom – Used for customer support, in-app chat, knowledge base (US).
  • Cloudflare – Used for content delivery (CDN), load balancing, and protecting Kululu from malicious traffic and DDoS attacks (EU - SE).
  • Paddle – Used for managing subscriptions, payment processing, invoicing, and acting as our merchant of record (EU - UK).
  • Google Ads – Used for running and tracking our own marketing and advertising campaigns. Not for retargeting users without consent (US).
  • Rewardful – Used for managing affiliate tracking, referrals, and calculating commission payments to our partners (CA).


4. Children’s Privacy

Kululu is intended for individuals who have reached the age of majority in their jurisdiction. We do not market to, or knowingly collect data from, children under 13.


5. How We Protect and Store Your Data

Your data is securely stored on Google Cloud (Firebase) servers located in the United States. We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your data. All storage and processing are done with appropriate safeguards as required by GDPR, CCPA, and the Australian Privacy Act. Please refer to our Security page for more information.
