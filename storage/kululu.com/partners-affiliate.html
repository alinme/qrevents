Frequently Asked Questions
How does it work?

It's pretty simple:

1. Sign-up to our affiliation program 🙋
Follow the link above to join our affiliation program.
‍‍
2. Wait for approval ⌛️

We'll check your application and in case it's relevant, you'll get a confirmation email to open an account on our referral program platform (where you track your earnings).
‍
3. Share your referral link with your audience 📣

You'll get a special link to Kululu you can share with your people. If they ended up purchasing a plan on our website using your link, you'll get credit for it. Don't worry if they don't make a purchase on the same session they entered your link - any purchase they make within 30 days will be attributed to you.

4. Enjoy your rewards 💰

You can find all the information regarding your attribution (clicks, purchases and more) made by your links in your dashboard, and withdraw the money you received.

How much money can I earn?

Well, it really depends on the size of your audience and how much you're going to push it.

For example, let's say you share Kululu (using your unique link) and 50 people ended up purchasing a 99$ plan. You'll get a commission of ~$1250 (25% of 50 x $99).

Assuming you have a constant traffic, that could be a nice "passive income" to your website/blog/social account.

There's no limit - the more people you refer, the more money you can get.

Payouts are automatically paid once you reach the threshold of $250.‍

How do I get paid?

You'll be paid via Wise or Bank/Wire Transfer. Payouts are automatically paid once you reach the threshold of $250.

Who is eligible to apply for the affiliate program?

Bloggers, influencers, service providers, or anyone interested in promoting Kululu to their audience are welcome. Please note that only genuine promotions will be approved. Retargeting Kululu through paid advertisements or coupon code promotion (unless approved by us) is prohibited and will disqualify any affiliate from earning.
