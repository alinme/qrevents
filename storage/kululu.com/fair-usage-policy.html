Fair Usage Policy

We at Kululu are on a mission to make public events 10x better.
To ensure we run our services smoothly for all our clients, you must comply with the following “Fair Usage” policies:
‍
1. It's your responsibility to make sure every media uploaded to your digital wall/shared album is safe, appropriate, and aligned with any law and commercial trademarks. 
‍
2. Kululu’s sole reason is to store your photos made by event running on Kululu. By no means, Kululu intended to be used as an all-purpose cloud drive (like Google Drive, Dropbox, etc.).

3. Our “Unlimited” plans are subject to a fair usage policy limited up to 10,000 media uploads and 5,000 participants per event. For most cases, that would be more than enough to manage a very large event. If you need more than that, please contact us so we adjust the limits.

4. Kululu’s team is eligible to adjust, delete or block any event or client that violates this policy without prior notice.
Please don't hesitate to contact us with any question regarding your event.
