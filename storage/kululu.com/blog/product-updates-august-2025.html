What’s New in Kululu – August 2025: Guest Consent & Content Filtering

Smarter features for safer, smoother events—here’s what’s new.

Ella Levy
Founder of Kululu
August 16, 2025
📕 Table of Contents
Why you should collect photos?
Why you should collect photos?

This month we’re rolling out new features to help you manage events with more control, better protection, and a smoother overall experience.

‍

Guest Consent Message

Planning to use event photos for marketing or social media? You can now display a friendly consent prompt before guests upload their images. This ensures you have clear permission to use their photos for business, promotional, or internal purposes—keeping things transparent, respectful, and compliant.

You can also add a link to your own terms and conditions or privacy policy, so guests know exactly how their photos may be used. This feature helps event organizers stay professional while protecting guest privacy. It’s particularly useful for companies running branded events, marketers collecting user-generated content, and anyone who wants to ensure they are using guest photos responsibly.

‍

Automatic Content Filtering

Kululu can now automatically detect and block adult, explicit, violent, or suggestive content before it appears in your event album. If something is flagged—whether it’s an image or a caption. It’s sent to a “Needs Approval” section for manual review.

This extra layer of protection helps keep your event space safe, welcoming, and free from unwanted surprises. The filter covers categories including sexually explicit content, racy or suggestive images, violent or gory visuals, and manipulated imagery—giving you peace of mind when managing large or public events.

‍

Custom Event Links

Your event link should be as memorable as your event. You can now personalize your link so it’s easier to share, easier to remember, and perfectly aligned with your brand, event type, or name. Need a little inspiration? Our AI link suggestion tool can help you find the perfect fit. So your guests can join and contribute without a hitch.

‍

Performance Improvements & Bug Fixes

We’ve also made improvements behind the scenes to give you a smoother experience:

Faster uploads

‍We’ve made uploading quicker, so you can add photos and videos without delays.

Smarter downloads

‍Media can now be downloaded in a more organized way—choose between all media, only media with captions, or just text posts.

Flexible captions

‍Captions can be hidden from the Photo Wall slideshow, edited after upload to refine descriptions, or removed entirely without re-uploading.

More stability

‍Behind the scenes, bug detection and fixes make your event workflow smoother and more reliable from start to finish.

‍

That’s a Wrap 🎉

This month’s updates are all about giving you more control, protecting your event space, and keeping your workflow smooth from start to finish. Whether you’re running a corporate event, a private celebration, or a large public gathering, these tools help you focus on what matters most—creating unforgettable memories without the worry.

We’d love to hear how these features work for you. Share your feedback, ideas, or questions with us anytime. We’re always listening and ready to make Kululu even better.
